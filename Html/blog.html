The Ultimate Realty Blog

After years of resisting blogging, I decided to take up the challenge to post some blog stories in 2018! I am a portrait and family photographer based in Essex. I do have quite a lot to say when it comes to photography so not sure why I have never made the big jump to create a blog! Here I will post some of my photoshoots, to give you an idea of my work but I will also share and talk about my passion for photography. I will also post some of my personal photos! Lucky you! So if you like what you see or if you want to talk about a post, or a subject related to photography, please feel free to do so. Although if you are looking for some tips on where to go on holiday in France, I can also help, not sure that will interest all readers but you never know! Enjoy my blog. Eric
